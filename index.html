Making Homework at Home Fun

Homework doesn't have to be a task you dread doing with your child. This app makes homework fun with rewards, interactive games and live feedback! See your child's teachers feedback and ask questions whenever you need something clearing up. Soon, your child will be begging for homework time!

What makes us special?

Save Time

It is quick and easy to log into our app where your child's homework will be set. No more looking around for paper and pen!

Quick Feedback

You and your child will be able to see comments made by their teacher as soon as they have marked the homework!

Earn Rewards

Everytime you log in and complete a task, your child earns stars to go towards a trophy! Which class will earn the most?
